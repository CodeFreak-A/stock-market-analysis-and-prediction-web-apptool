About Us

We are a dedicated team of students from the Institute of Technology Patna, representing Group No. 26, passionate about exploring the dynamic world of finance through our Capstone project. Comprising Krrish Raj, Priyanshu Nandan, Aditi Mandal, Ravi Raj, and Vibhas Prabhanjan, we bring together diverse backgrounds and expertise to delve into the realm of Stock Market Analysis and Prediction.

Our mission is to develop a cutting-edge web application that empowers investors with insightful data-driven analysis and accurate predictions, enhancing their decision-making process in the volatile stock market environment. Through meticulous research, robust algorithms, and innovative technology, we aim to provide users with actionable insights and forecasts to navigate the complexities of financial markets effectively.

Driven by a shared commitment to excellence and continuous learning, we strive to deliver a solution that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of our stakeholders. With a blend of creativity, technical prowess, and a deep understanding of financial markets, we are poised to make a significant impact in the field of stock market analysis through our Capstone project.
